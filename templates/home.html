Our Story

We were both living in Philadelphia in 2015 when two of our closest friends started apartment hunting and asked us to join them for a quick bite to eat at Amada in Old City. After catching up with our respective friends (read: ignoring each other), we quickly found we had a lot in common. In the weeks to come, and several apartment tours later, our friends had found the perfect house, and we had a date on the books (which was apparently the plan all along...)

Just over two years later and after a day of adventure, Alex proposed during a quiet night at home over hot cocoa, with our puppy Lady sleeping on the couch next to us. Kylie excitedly answered "Obviously!" and so began a new chapter of our life together.
